About

I am a Master's student in Business Analytics at Anna University, Chennai, where I am an ICCR Scholar. After four years in humanitarian programs across Afghanistan — from data collection and field operations with INTERSOS and OXFAM — I'm now focused on turning field data into clear, actionable insights using Python, SQL, Power BI, and Azure.

I earned my Bachelor's in Urban and Regional Planning from Kabul Polytechnic University and have built projects like an interactive NGO survey dashboard and an automated data quality system (both on GitHub) that cut errors and save time for teams on the ground.

I believe technology can make humanitarian work smarter and more effective. Now I am actively looking for data analyst or field operations roles in NGOs. If you have any opening or just want to chat about opportunities, feel free to reach out!
